QUICK STEPS TO FIND DATABASE ACCESS:

1. Click "All Projects" in left sidebar
2. Click on your project/cluster name
3. Look for "Security" in left sidebar (in project view)
4. Click "Database Access" under Security
5. Click "Add New Database User"

ALTERNATIVE:
- Look for "Database" or "Clusters" in sidebar
- Click on your cluster
- Find "Security" or "Database Access"

KEY: You need to be in PROJECT view, not ORGANIZATION view!
